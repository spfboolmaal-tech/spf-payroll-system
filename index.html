🔗 Dejinta Server-ka Wadaagga (LAN Server)

Sida loo isticmaalo (server.js):
1. Hal kombuyutar ku qor: node server.js
2. LAN (gudaha, internet la'aan): kombuyutarrada kale geli IP-ga kombuyutarkaas, tusaale http://192.168.1.10:3000
3. Online: server.js + faylka HTML-ka ku shub Azure App Service (ama meel kale), geli URL-kiisa
4. Ku dhufo "Test Connection". Markaa, dhammaan kombuyutarrada isla xog ayey arkayaan, oo si toos ah (20 sek.) waa is-update gareeyaan.
Haddii server-ka aan la heli karin (offline), nidaamku wuxuu sii shaqeynayaa isagoo isticmaalaya xogtii kaydsanayd ee gudaha (offline mode).

🖐 Dejinta SecuGen Fingerprint Scanner

Sida loo isticmaalo SecuGen Scanner-ka:
1. Scanner-ka USB ku xir kombuyutarka oo hubi in driver-ku shaqeynayo.
2. Hubi in barnaamijka SecuGen WebAPI / SgiBioSrv uu socdo (service-ka background-ka ah).
3. Mar koowaad, browser-ka ku fur tab cusub oo tag https://localhost:8000 (ama port-ka aad isticmaaleyso), kadibna ogolow (Accept/Continue) digniinta certificate-ka - tani waa lagama maarmaan si browser-ku u ogolaado xiriirka.
4. Halkan geli URL-ka WebAPI-ga (caadi ahaan https://localhost:8000), kadibna riix "Test Scanner".
5. Haddii uu ku guuleysto, tag "Diiwaan Gelinta" ➜ tab-ka Fingerprint ➜ "Scan Fingerprint" si aad farta sax ah uga qaaddo.
Haddii xiriirku fashilmo: hubi WebAPI-gu inuu socdo, hubi port-ka (8000 ama 8443), iyo in cert-ka la ogolaaday sida lagu sharaxay sare.

💾 Kaydinta Xogta (Backup & Restore)

Xogtaadu si toos ah ayey ugu kaydsantaa device-kan (IndexedDB) mar kasta oo aad xog cusub gelisid ama wax ka beddesho. Si aad ugu hubsato in xogtu aanay mar dambe lumin, waxaa lagula talinayaa inaad mar walba soo dejiso (download) backup file, oo aad kaydiso meel ammaan ah (USB, Google Drive, iwm). Haddii xogtu mar dambe luundo, fayl-kaas backup ah waxaad ku soo celin kartaa.
